Helpdesk support

We are here to assist you with any inquiries related to GIS service publication, as well as the creation of maps and applications in ArcGIS Portal and other GIS applications at EEA. Our dedicated Helpdesk channel is available to provide comprehensive support and address any questions you may have.

Helpdesk channel available at discomap@eionet.europa.eu

Our team of experienced professionals is well-versed in the intricacies of GIS technologies and workflows. Whether you need guidance on publishing services, configuring maps, or developing interactive applications, we are here to ensure your success. We understand the importance of seamless data management and effective utilization of GIS tools within the context of EEA's goals and objectives. By leveraging our expertise and extensive knowledge of ArcGIS technologies, we aim to empower you to make the most out of the GIS resources available.

Please don't hesitate to reach out to our Helpdesk with any concerns or inquiries you may have. We are committed to providing timely and accurate assistance to support your GIS tasks and contribute to the success of your projects.
